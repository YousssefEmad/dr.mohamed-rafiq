How We Care

Step-by-Step Care for Vascular Health

At Dr. Mohamed Rafiq’s Vascular Clinic, every patient receives a thorough and personalized approach. From precise diagnosis to advanced minimally invasive treatments, we ensure your circulation health is in expert hands.

01

Consultation & Diagnosis

We start with a detailed consultation and Doppler ultrasound to identify the exact cause of your condition, whether varicose veins, blood clots, or arterial blockage.

02

Personalized Treatment

Depending on your diagnosis, we provide the most suitable treatment — from laser vein therapy and injections to advanced endovascular procedures — all with minimal discomfort.

03

Follow-up & Recovery

After treatment, we monitor your recovery closely with follow-up visits and lifestyle guidance to prevent recurrence and ensure lasting vascular health.
