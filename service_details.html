Overview:

Varicose veins are enlarged, twisted veins that appear under the skin, usually in the legs. They are caused by weakened vein walls and valves, leading to poor blood circulation. At Dr. Mohamed Rafiq’s Vascular Clinic, we use advanced diagnostic tools and minimally invasive techniques to relieve pain, swelling, and cosmetic concerns.

Our goal is to restore proper blood flow and improve your comfort and mobility. With the latest laser and injection-based treatments, patients can expect faster recovery and minimal discomfort.

How it Works?

The treatment process begins with an accurate diagnosis using Doppler ultrasound to assess vein function. Depending on your condition, Dr. Rafiq will recommend the most effective solution — whether endovenous laser therapy, sclerotherapy, or minor surgical procedures.

01. Consultation & Diagnosis

Comprehensive evaluation using Doppler ultrasound to identify vein issues.

02. Personalized Treatment

Tailored approach using laser, injection, or micro-surgery techniques.

03. Quick Recovery

Return to normal activity with minimal downtime and lasting results.

Why Choose This Treatment?

Treating varicose veins is not just about appearance — it’s about improving circulation, preventing complications like ulcers or blood clots, and enhancing overall leg health.
